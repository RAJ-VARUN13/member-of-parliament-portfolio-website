Vision & Developmental Agenda

A strategic blueprint for Jharkhand's growth, focused on educational infrastructure, tribal heritage preservation, and sustainable grassroots progress.

Educational Revolution

Strengthening higher education and vocational skills to establish Jharkhand as a regional knowledge hub. Dr. Varma advocates modern school networks and accessible university systems.

  • Expand professional engineering and management programs.
  • Support vocational nursing training institutes in rural Latehar.
  • Enhance secondary education with digital learning platforms.

Tribal Heritage & Sarna Culture

Preserving and celebrating the distinct cultural identities, languages, and tribal traditions of Jharkhand. Focused on protecting indigenous Sarna roots and local traditions.

  • Standardize and document local tribal dialects and literature.
  • Sponsor community conventions in Ranchi and Latehar.
  • Secure developmental grants for historic regional Sarna sites.

Rural Health Infrastructure

Fostering decentralization in healthcare to ensure quality medical facilities reach the remote corners and villages of Latehar and surrounding districts.

  • Boost nursing and paramedic training seats via vocational networks.
  • Set up mobile clinical camps for weekly tribal health screenings.
  • Support digital healthcare databases in collaboration with AIIMS.

Youth & Vocational Training

Building specialized training networks that align Jharkhand's youth with modern industrial and service sector employment opportunities.

  • Establish modern computing and coding training desks in schools.
  • Build tie-ups with national companies for apprenticeship modules.
  • Foster youth entrepreneurship through local startup microfinance.

Industrial & Resource Strategy

Advocating for sustainable mining policies that protect forest ecosystems while securing fair local employment, resource royalties, and safety standards.

  • Coordinate with the Parliamentary Committee on Coal, Mines, and Steel.
  • Ensure corporate social responsibility (CSR) directly funds local villages.
  • Modernize mine safety and rehabilitation operations.

Digital Governance & Reforms

Enabling citizens to access government representatives, welfare schemes, and legislative functions seamlessly through transparent digital channels.

  • Connect villages through e-grievance registration programs.
  • Integrate parliamentary work updates on interactive public portals.
  • Train regional party workers to facilitate e-governance applications.
